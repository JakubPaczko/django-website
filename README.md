# Purpledid

Purpledid is a vibrant social media platform designed to bring people together through shared interests and engaging content. With a focus on communities and meaningful interactions, Purpledid offers a unique and user-friendly experience.

## Features

### Core Features
- **Adding Posts**: Share your thoughts, ideas, and updates with the community.
- **Commenting**: Engage in discussions by leaving comments on posts.
- **Communities**: Join or create communities to connect with like-minded individuals.
- **Liking**:
  - Like posts to show appreciation for the content.
  - Like comments to support thoughtful discussions.
- **Following**:
  - Follow communities to stay updated on topics that interest you.
  - Follow users to see their latest posts in your feed.

### Upcoming Features
- **Messaging**: Directly communicate with other users through private messages. *(Planned)*
